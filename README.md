### Project Overview

Created an interactive HR dashboard using Power BI and SQL to analyze employee data such as ethnicity ,gender diversity, and 
department-wise distribution etc.The dashboard helps HR teams monitor workforce trends and make data-driven decisions.

### Tool Used

Data - HR Data with over 22000 rows from the year 2000 to 2020.</br>
Data Cleaning & Analysis - MySQL Workbench</br>
Data Visualization - PowerBI

### Exploratory Data Analysis

EDA involved exploring the hr data to anaswer key questions,such as:-

Q1. What is the gender breakdown of employees in the company?</br>
Q2. What is the race/ethnicity breakdown of employees in the company?</br>
Q3. How many employees work at headquarters versus remote locations?</br>
Q4. What is the average length of employment for employees who have been terminated?</br>
Q5. How does the gender distribution vary across departments and job titles?</br>
Q6. What is the distribution of job titles across the company?</br>
Q7. What is the distribution of employees across locations by state?</br>
Q8. What is the tenure distribution for each department?

### Summary of Findings

There are more male employees</br>
White race is the most dominant while Native Hawaiian and American Indian are the least dominant.</br>
A large number of employees work at the headquarters versus remotely.</br>
The average length of employment for terminated employees is around 10 years.</br>
The gender distribution across departments is fairly balanced but there are generally more male than female employees.</br>
A large number of employees come from the state of Ohio.

### Limitations
Some records had negative ages and these were excluded during querying(967 records). Ages used were 18 years and above.
